Scheduling Reminders

The following mail will get resent to the user at 8:00 AM on 30th February 2020.


Subject : Reminder
Body : Remind me about this again tomorrow morning. @action mail @date 2020-02-30T08:00


Now if you want the reminder to be repeated daily, you simply send another mail with the same subject and a repeat token.


Subject : Reminder
Body : @repeat daily


If you want the scheduled reminder to go to someone@something.com instead of yourself, then you simply add that email id in the recipient list.


Subject : Reminder
Body : @to someone@something.com


Note that if a valid mail recipient is available, lewinsky will not send the reminder back to the user which was its default recipient. You will explicitly need to add your email id to the list if you want to continue receiving the reminders.


The Forgetful Boyfriend

Say its 3 AM and you suddenly realise that you have forgot your girlfriend's birthday. Her phone's switched off. Now you have to buy her an expensive gift tomorrow. You want to be reminded about it at 6 PM when you leave office. Send this mail to note@mylewinsky.com :


Subject : Buy Gift
Body : Buy a shit expensive gift or you will get no cookies! @action mail @date 2020-02-30T18:00


Now lets say you think you might not check the mail since you might be on road and you want to be called to remind you the same. Send another mail to note@mylewinsky.com :


Subject : Buy Gift
Body : @action call @to +91xxxxxxxxxx


Since the subject is the same, you need not specify the body. The body of the last message will be played back to you in the call. At the same time as was specified in the last mail.


The Dog Killer

You are woken from your sleep by the neighbour's barking dog in the middle of the night. You decide to kill it the first thing in the morning


Subject : Murder
Body : Kill the neighbour's dog @date 2020-02-30T08:00 @action mail @action call @to +91xxxxxxxxxx


Oh! You forgot to remind yourself to bury it.


Subject : Murder
Body : Remember to bury the furball


And since it is a big dog with great big teeth, you might want a friend's help as well.


Subject : Murder
Body : @to myself@myself.com @to friend@friend.com


Now you can go back to sleep...
